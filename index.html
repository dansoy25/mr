Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and the way we work.

What services does M&R MECH DEVELOPMENT provide?

We provide comprehensive mechanical, electrical, and specialized engineering services including Air Conditioning (LG-accredited), Electrical Works, Fire Protection, Mechanical Works, Plumbing & Sanitary, and Diesel Generator Set maintenance.

Are you certified and registered?

Yes. We are PCAB certified (No. 44715), DTI registered, and our founder Engr. Marc Neil R. Padagdag is a registered Mechanical Engineer with memberships in PSVARE and PSME Mandaue Chapter. SEC Certificate No. 2025100222652-31.

How long have you been in business?

M&R MECH DEVELOPMENT Inc. was established on November 27, 2017. Over the years, we've successfully completed numerous projects for major clients including Makati Development Corporation, Ayala Land, and leading commercial establishments.

Do you provide maintenance services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance services for all systems we install — AC maintenance, generator set support, fire protection inspections, and electrical system checks. We offer scheduled preventive maintenance plans tailored to your facility.

Which regions do you serve?

Headquartered in Cebu City, we serve clients across the Philippines. Past projects span Cebu (Cebu Business Park, IT Park, Mandaue), Lapu-Lapu, and as far as Tacloban City.

Use the contact form below, email mrengineering718@gmail.com, or call +63 918 453 3834. We typically respond within one business day with next steps for site survey or scoping.
